The Process

I did in depth research into monograms, visual marques, and wordmarks as well as the best way to represent myself as a brand. I did a great deal of experimentation and development before refining designs. I then created my final brand, a set of Brand Guidelines and a variety of touch points such as business cards and writing pads.

The Final Product

I created a representative brand for myself, a set of Brand Guidelines and a variety of professional touch points featuring the brand.

Reflection

I gained a lot of skills whilst creating my brand, and learnt first-hand just how much work goes into creating visual marques, monograms and wordmarks. I also learnt the processes that are involved in creating a brand, which I will refer to in future.
